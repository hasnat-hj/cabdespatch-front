Who We Are

Our Story
CabDespatch

We're very proud of the fact that we're "Built by a Cab Company, For a Cab Company"... but what does that really mean? Firstly, all the founders, directors and shareholders of CabDespatch have worked extensively in various roles in the cab industry so we really understand the industry.

Be it drivers complaining of feeding, customers complaining of being overcharged or customers complaining about rude staff, we've seen it all! Sharing office space with one of East Kent's busiest circuits really ensures that we're aware of any changes in the industry and keep up-to-date with all the goings on that affect you.

Choose Us With Confidence

Friendly Support

We ensure that if you need to speak to us, it'll always be to a friendly human & never an answering robot. Our staff are able to take ownership of any issue, from top-up-training to complex technical issues.

Modular System

We visit cab companies nationwide and one thing has stood out above all else, all companies are different! That's why with CabDespatch, we adopt a modular system so you only add features you need.

Working Together

It's important for our customers to know that whatever their requirements, we'll do our upmost to work together to achieve a solution. For a special requirement, just give us a call!
